#

This repository is a **personal knowledge base** where I document my journey through **real-world labs and CTF challenges**.  
It includes **detailed step-by-step write-ups** for:

- **TryHackMe rooms** , **HackTheBox machines** , **Root-Me challenges** and **Custom Labs** ‚Äì homemade challenges and notes from experiments.

Each write-up is focused on **explaining the methodology**, not just the solution.  
The goal is to **share techniques, tools, and thought processes** for offensive security.

## **Structure**

- **`/tryhackme/`** ‚Üí All TryHackMe write-ups.
- **`/hackthebox/`** ‚Üí Detailed HTB machines and challenges.
- **`/root-me/`** ‚Üí Notes and solutions for Root-Me challenges.
- **`/labs/`** ‚Üí Personal or custom CTF environments.
- **`/cheatsheets/`** ‚Üí Quick notes on tools and techniques.
